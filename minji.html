What Minji does
Six ways she keeps members learning
Personalized AI lessons
Minji builds short lessons and practice drills that match your current level, generated fresh every time.
Smart personal vocabulary
Save any word or phrase, and Minji turns your saved list into quizzes built just for you.
/vocab_add
TOPIK-style exams
Preparing for the official test, or just want to check where you stand? Sit a real exam right in chat.
/exam
Live conversation
Chat with Minji like a native speaker. She keeps track of context, corrects mistakes, and helps you speak up without the fear of getting it wrong.
RPG-style progress
Every finished task earns XP and pushes you up a level. Track it all on your profile — just mind your energy, which refills over time.
K-culture guide
Ask any time for K-pop track picks, Korean food to try, or a drama to start next — Minji teaches the language and the culture around it.
Why admins add Minji
Zero setup
Invite her and she's ready to go — no configuration, no database, no extra permissions to grant.
Slash commands only
Minji runs entirely on modern slash commands, so she never spams your channels or needs admin rights.
Built for engagement
Quizzes, levels and drama talk give members a steady reason to come back to your text channels.
